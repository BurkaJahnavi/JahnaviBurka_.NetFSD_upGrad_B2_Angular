Her Journey in Tech

Discovering Computer Science

After my Intermediate, we had to choose our branch based on our scores. During the holidays, I was confused and had no seniors to guide me. Many people gave different suggestions, but I learned that CSE was usually given to students with good grades. I also remembered how much I enjoyed creating web pages in Intermediate. That interest made my decision clear — I chose Computer Science, and that’s how my tech journey began.

Building My Foundation

I learned core subjects like Data Structures and DBMS while improving my logic and problem-solving skills. Moving from C to Java strengthened my programming foundation.

My First Internship Experience

During my internship, I built projects like a calculator, to-do list, and Connect 4 game. It improved my practical skills and confidence

Working on Machine Learning Projects

I worked on GANs and GNNs to handle missing data and predict corn yields. This project challenged me to think deeper about data and model performance.

Preparing for Placements & Beyond

I focused on SQL, .NET, and technical interview preparation. This stage helped me refine my skills and prepare for career opportunities.
